TNPSC Group I 2025–26 – Student Stipend Programme

As part of the TNPSC Group I Main Examination 2025–26 preparation, a stipend distribution programme was conducted for more than 525 candidates who are undergoing training at the Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy.

Date: 29.10.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, works with the objective of supporting socially and economically underprivileged sections of society through various social service initiatives without discrimination.

The TNPSC Group I Preliminary Examination for 2025 was conducted in June. Following the release of the results, the Main Examination has been scheduled for the first week of December 2025. Continuous mock test training sessions for candidates who cleared the prelims have been conducted both offline and online since 15.09.2025.

Training sessions are being handled by former Manidhanaeyam students who previously cleared the Group I examination. A special one-day workshop was also conducted to improve candidates’ memory skills and handwriting for the Main Examination.

Study materials and recorded video lectures for the Main Examination have been uploaded on the Manidhanaeyam official website for the benefit of students.

In addition, the Founder of Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy, along with former Tamil Nadu Director General of Police (DGP) Mr. Alexander, distributed a stipend of ₹10,000 each to more than 525 candidates during programmes held on 26.10.2025 and 29.10.2025.

This information was released through an official press note by the Manidhanaeyam Foundation.
