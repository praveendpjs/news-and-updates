TNPSC Group I Main Examination Result 2024–25

In the TNPSC Group I Main Examination 2024–25, a total of 17 candidates (8 women and 9 men) trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have successfully cleared the examination.

Date: 14.03.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, works for the upliftment of socially and economically disadvantaged communities through continuous educational and social welfare initiatives.

Over the last 19 years, more than 4,000 students trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have been selected to prestigious positions such as IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, Indian Forest Service, TNPSC Group I, II, IIA, Sub‑Inspector and Assistant Engineer posts.

To bridge learning gaps caused by the COVID‑19 pandemic, online coaching for both UPSC and TNPSC examinations has been made available to students.

The TNPSC Group I 2024 notification for 90 vacancies was released on 28.03.2024. The Preliminary Examination was conducted on 13.07.2024 and the results were declared on 02.09.2024.

The Main Examination was held on 10th, 11th, 12th and 13th December 2024, and the results were announced on 14.03.2025. A total of 17 candidates have qualified.

All successful candidates will receive free mock interview training, model interview sessions and current affairs study material at the Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Training Centre.

This information was officially announced by the Founder of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
