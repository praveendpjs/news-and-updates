CAPF Assistant Commandant Interview Result 2024–25

In the UPSC CAPF (Central Armed Police Forces) Assistant Commandant Interview 2024–25, eight students trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have successfully cleared the final selection.

Date: 14.06.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, continues to work for the upliftment of socially and economically underprivileged communities.

Over the past 20 years, more than 4,420 students trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have been selected to prestigious positions including IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, Indian Forest Service and senior TNPSC posts.

The UPSC CAPF notification for 459 vacancies was released on 24.04.2024. The written examination was conducted on 04.08.2024, followed by Physical Efficiency Test and Medical Examination on 11.12.2024 and 12.12.2024.

The first phase of interviews was held from 05.05.2025 to 23.05.2025 and the second phase from 26.05.2025 to 06.06.2025.

A total of 21 candidates from Manidhanaeyam appeared for the interview. The final results were announced on 13.06.2025, in which 8 candidates successfully secured the post of CAPF Assistant Commandant.

Among them, Aravindh Eswaryankutty from Kerala secured All India Rank 69, S.D. Suryanarayanan from Dindigul secured Rank 96, and R. Thavasvi Arunachalam from Sivagangai secured Rank 120.

The Founder of Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy, congratulated all the successful candidates.
