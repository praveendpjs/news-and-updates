TNPSC Group I 2024–25 – Service Allocation

In the TNPSC Group I Examination 2024–25, a total of 27 candidates trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have successfully received government service postings.

Date: 09.05.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, continues to work for the upliftment of socially and economically underprivileged communities through various service initiatives.

Over the last 20 years, more than 4,420 students trained at Manidhanaeyam have been selected to prestigious roles such as IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, Indian Forest Service, TNPSC Group I, II, IIA, Sub-Inspector and Assistant Engineer positions.

The TNPSC Group I 2024–25 notification was released for 96 vacancies and the selection process was conducted in three stages.

Based on the final merit list released on 09.05.2025, 27 Manidhanaeyam students secured postings in various departments.
