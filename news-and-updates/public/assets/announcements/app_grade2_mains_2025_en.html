Assistant Public Prosecutor Grade-II Main Examination Result 2025

In the TNPSC Assistant Public Prosecutor Grade-II Main Examination 2025, conducted jointly with the Tamil Nadu & Puducherry Bar Council and Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy, a total of 22 advocates have successfully cleared the examination.

Date: 18.11.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, works for the upliftment of socially and economically underprivileged communities.

So far, a total of 298 candidates trained through free coaching jointly by the Bar Council and Manidhanaeyam have been appointed, including 267 Civil Judges, 9 District Judges and 22 Assistant Public Prosecutors.

As per TNPSC notification, the Preliminary Examination for 51 APP Grade-II vacancies was conducted on 22.02.2025 and the results were declared on 24.04.2025. The Main Examination was held from 26th to 30th July 2025 and the final results were published on 18.11.2025.

All the candidates who cleared the Main Examination will receive free mock interview training conducted jointly by the Bar Council and Manidhanaeyam.

This information was jointly announced by the Chairman of the Bar Council, Mr. P.S. Amalraj, and the Chairman of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
