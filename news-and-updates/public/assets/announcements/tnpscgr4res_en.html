TNPSC Group IV Result 2025

In the TNPSC Group IV Examination 2025, more than 232 students who studied at Manidhanaeyam have secured high marks and are likely to receive government job allocations.

Date: 24.10.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Trust was established in 2005 and has been functioning with the aim of supporting socially and economically underprivileged people. It carries out various social service activities without discrimination based on caste or religion.

The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission released notification for 4662 vacancies for Group IV on 25.04.2025. The examination was held on 12.07.2025, and the result ranking list was published on 22.10.2025.

Based on the ranking list, students who trained through the Manidhanaeyam website and those who previously attempted competitive exams multiple times without success — in total more than 232 students — have now secured high scores and are likely to receive job postings in the upcoming counseling process.

This information was officially announced by the Founder of Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
