Date: 28.08.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation was established in 2005 with the objective of supporting socially and economically disadvantaged communities. It functions without discrimination based on caste or religion and actively engages in various social service initiatives.

The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission released a notification for 70 Group I vacancies on 01.04.2025. The Preliminary Examination was conducted on 15.06.2025, and the results were announced on 28.08.2025. Based on the results, 67 students trained at Manidhanaeyam have qualified for the Main Examination.

These successful candidates include students currently working in government services, previous Main Examination and Interview trainees, and students trained through the Manidhanaeyam online platform.

Main Examination Coaching with Financial Support

In addition to Manidhanaeyam students, other candidates who cleared the Preliminary Examination are also eligible to register for Main Examination coaching with financial assistance.

The full syllabus and recorded video classes required for Main Examination preparation have been uploaded online, allowing students to continue training from home.

This information was officially announced by the Founder of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
