UPSC Civil Services Interview Result 2024–25

In the UPSC Civil Services Interview 2024–25, a total of 35 students trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have successfully cleared the final stage, including 23 men and 12 women.

Date: 22.04.2025

Among the top performers, B. Sivasandran from Chennai secured All India Rank 23, R. Monica from Chennai secured Rank 39, and Sreerushad from Ramanathapuram secured Rank 52.

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, continues to empower students from socially and economically underprivileged backgrounds.

Free coaching was provided through television and online platforms for the Preliminary Examination. Monthly stipends were provided for qualified candidates, and full support including mock interviews, personal mentorship, free travel to Delhi, accommodation and interview preparation facilities were offered.

The Founder of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy, congratulated all the successful candidates.
