Assistant Public Prosecutor Grade-II Preliminary Examination Result 2024–25

In the TNPSC Assistant Public Prosecutor Grade-II Preliminary Examination 2024–25, conducted jointly with the Tamil Nadu & Puducherry Bar Council and Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy, a total of 34 candidates have successfully cleared the examination.

Date: 26.04.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, continues to serve socially and economically underprivileged communities through various educational initiatives.

So far, 261 Civil Judges, 9 District Judges and 19 Assistant Public Prosecutors — a total of 288 candidates — have been successfully appointed after undergoing free training conducted jointly by the Bar Council and Manidhanaeyam.

As per the TNPSC notification, the Preliminary Examination for 51 APP Grade-II vacancies was conducted on 22.02.2025 and the results were declared on 23.04.2025, in which 34 candidates qualified.

Free coaching for the Main Examination will be conducted for all qualified candidates.

This information was jointly announced by the Chairman of the Bar Council, Mr. P.S. Amalraj, and the Chairman of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
