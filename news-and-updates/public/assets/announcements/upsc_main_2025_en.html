UPSC Civil Services Main Examination Result 2025

In the UPSC Civil Services Main Examination 2025, a total of 93 students trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy (63 men and 30 women) have successfully qualified for the Interview stage.

Date: 12.11.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, continues to serve socially and economically underprivileged communities through free educational initiatives.

Over the past 20 years, more than 4,420 students trained at Manidhanaeyam have been selected to top positions including IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, Indian Forest Service and TNPSC Group I, II and IIA services.

For UPSC 2025, the notification for 1,129 vacancies was released on 22.01.2025. The Preliminary Examination was held on 25.05.2025 and the Main Examination was conducted on 22, 23, 24 August and 30, 31 August 2025. The results were declared on 11.11.2025.

All the 93 qualified candidates will receive free Interview training including mock interviews, expert mentoring, study materials, daily training sessions, personal guidance, free flight tickets to Delhi, accommodation, food and professional attire.

This information was officially announced by the Founder of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
