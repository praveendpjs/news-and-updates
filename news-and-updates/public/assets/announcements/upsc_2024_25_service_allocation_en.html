UPSC Civil Services Examination 2024–2025 – Service Allocation

In the 2024–2025 Civil Services Examination, out of 35 candidates trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy, 23 male and 12 female students have cleared the exam. Among them, 31 candidates have already received service allocation — 9 to the Indian Administrative Service (IAS), 1 to the Indian Foreign Service (IFS), 2 to the Indian Police Service (IPS) and 9 to the Indian Revenue Service (IRS – IT & related cadres). Service allocation for 4 candidates is still pending.

Date: 06.08.2025

Through Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy, students from across Tamil Nadu have been trained with the vision of enabling them to secure top All-India services such as IAS and IPS. Over the past 20 years, more than 4,420 students have been selected to prestigious posts in services including IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, Indian Forest Service, as well as high posts in state services through the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission examinations such as Group I, II, IIA (DC, DSP, DRO, DEO, CTO), Sub-Inspector and Assistant Engineer (Highways, Rural Development & Panchayat Raj, PWD – Civil, Electrical, CJ, DJ, APP).

The Union Public Service Commission conducts the Civil Services Examination every year for recruitment to IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS and various other central services. The examination is held in three stages – Preliminary, Main and Interview.

In TNPSC Group IV services (including posts such as VAO, Junior Assistant – Security/Non-Security, Bill Collector Grade I, Field Surveyor, Draftsman, Typist and Steno-Typist), more than 42,400 candidates from 219 community categories trained by Manidhanaeyam have so far been selected and are in service.

For the year 2024, the Union Public Service Commission issued a notification on 14.02.2024 for 1,056 vacancies including IAS and IPS. The list of candidates who cleared the Preliminary Examination and became eligible to appear for the Main Examination was then published.

Support Provided to Candidates

  1. Monthly financial assistance was given to students appearing for the Main Examination. All candidates who cleared the Main Examination were given free model interview training at the Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Training Centre, along with several additional facilities at no cost.
  2. Candidates appearing for the interview were provided free flight tickets to Delhi and accommodation arrangements in New Delhi.

Following the interviews held in Delhi, service allocation orders were issued on 05.08.2025. Out of the 35 Manidhanaeyam candidates, 23 male and 12 female students have been allotted services. They have been selected to the following services: 9 to IAS, 1 to IFS, 2 to IPS, 9 to IRS (IT & related), 2 to IRMS, 1 to IDAS, 1 to ICLS, 1 to ICAS, 2 to IIS, 1 to IP & TAFS, 1 to IPoS and 1 to ITS. Service allocation for 4 other candidates is pending.

The Founder of Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy, has extended his congratulations to all the candidates who have received service allocation.
