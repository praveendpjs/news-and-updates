UPSC Indian Forest Service (IFoS) Interview Result 2024–25

In the UPSC Indian Forest Service (IFoS) Interview 2024–25, three students trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have successfully cleared the final stage.

Date: 20.05.2025

The UPSC issued the notification on 14.02.2024 to fill 150 IFoS vacancies through the 2024 examination.

All candidates who cleared the Main Examination received free daily mock interview training at the Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Training Centre. Online training was also provided for candidates unable to attend in person.

The selected candidates and their All India Ranks are: J. Bibisha from Kanniyakumari – Rank 88, P. Plassy Srijil from Tirunelveli – Rank 110, and P. Arun Srinivas from Chennai – Rank 125.

The Chairman of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy, congratulated all the successful candidates.
