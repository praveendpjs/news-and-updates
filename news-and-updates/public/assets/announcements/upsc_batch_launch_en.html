A total of 71 students have passed - 57 male students and 14 female students who studied at the Manidhaneyam Centre. Free accommodation and food will be provided to the successful candidates.

Date: 12.06.2025

In order to help people who are backward in social and economic status, "Manidhaneyam Foundation" was started and has been functioning since 2005. Without discrimination of caste or religion, it has been performing various social services so that people from all sections can benefit.

In the last 20 years, more than 4,420 students have been successful in positions such as I.A.S, I.P.S, I.F.S, I.R.S, Indian Forest Service, Group-I, II, IIA conducted by Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission, Sub-Inspector, A.E (Highways, Rural Development & Panchayat Raj, PWD Civil & Electrical).

So far, more than 44,200 people have been successful and are working in Group-4 positions (VAO, Junior Assistant, Bill Collector, Field Surveyor, Draftsman, Typist, Steno-Typist) in 219 caste categories.

For the year 2025, 1129 vacant positions for UPSC were announced on 22.01.2025. The preliminary examination was held on 25.05.2025. Results were released on 12.06.2025.

In order for candidates all over India to receive UPSC training from home, training for the preliminary examination was provided through television and the internet.

Including those who wrote the exam last time and those who wrote the preliminary exam this year: A total of 71 people have passed - 57 male students and 14 female students. Among them, 16 people have passed through online training for two years.

For the Main Examination and Interview:

  • Direct classes
  • Regular practice tests
  • Study notes
  • Expert panel advice
  • Guidance from former successful students

The Founder-Chairman of Manidhaneyam Foundation Mr. Saidai Duraisamy has announced that free accommodation and food will be provided based on merit to those who pass this examination.

Successful students should bring their:

  • Passport size photo
  • Admit card for preliminary examination

With these, registration can be done in person, by phone or online at Manidhaneyam I.A.S Training Centre from 13.06.2025 onwards.
