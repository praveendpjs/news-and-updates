UPSC Main Examination 2025 – Stipend Distribution Programme

Under the initiative of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation founded by Mr. Saidai Duraisamy, a stipend distribution programme was conducted for more than 340 candidates who are appearing for the UPSC Main Examination.

Date: 16.07.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, continues to serve socially and economically underprivileged communities through various educational and social welfare programmes.

Over the past 20 years, more than 4,420 students trained at Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy have been selected to prestigious positions such as IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, Indian Forest Service and various TNPSC Group I, II, IIA, Sub-Inspector and Assistant Engineer posts.

The UPSC Preliminary Examination results were declared on 11.06.2025, and the Main Examination is scheduled to commence from 22.08.2025.

More than 340 qualified candidates participated in the event held on 16.07.2025 at 9:30 AM at the Manidhanaeyam office in Chennai and received a stipend of ₹15,000 each.

The event was attended by retired senior police officer Mr. Alexander and the Founder of Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy. They interacted with the candidates and motivated them to face the Main Examination with confidence. Mr. Duraisamy also shared important guidance on time management, physical health and mental well-being during the examination period.

This information was officially announced by the Founder of the Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
