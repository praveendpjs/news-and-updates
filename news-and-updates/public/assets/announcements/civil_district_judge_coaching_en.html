Free Civil Judge & District Judge Prelims Coaching 2025–26

The Tamil Nadu and Puducherry Bar Council, in association with Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy, will be conducting free preliminary coaching for the Civil Judge examination conducted by the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission and the District Judge examination conducted by the High Court.

Date: 16.08.2025

The Manidhanaeyam Foundation, established in 2005, continues to work for the upliftment of socially and economically underprivileged sections through educational and social service initiatives.

So far, 267 Civil Judges, 9 District Judges, and 22 Government Assistant Public Prosecutors — a total of 298 candidates — have been successfully appointed after undergoing free training through this initiative.

Advocates who wish to attend this free coaching programme must first register online at www.mntfreeias.com and then submit the physical application form at the Manidhanaeyam Free IAS Academy office.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Civil Judge: Minimum 3 years of legal practice
  • District Judge: Minimum 7 years of legal practice

The coaching classes will commence from 21.08.2025 and will be conducted in morning and evening sessions, as announced by Tamil Nadu and Puducherry Bar Council Chairman Mr. P.S. Amalraj and the Founder of Manidhanaeyam Foundation, Mr. Saidai Duraisamy.
